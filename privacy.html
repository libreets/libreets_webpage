Libreets – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Libreets collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

Effective date: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following types of data:

  • Email address provided during account registration.
  • Public profile data such as username, description (bio), and avatar.
  • Content you create inside the Libreets application (e.g., posts, audio, descriptions, etc.).
  • Basic technical diagnostic information (such as anonymized error logs and performance metrics).

2. Why We Collect This Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To display your profile and content to other Libreets users.
  • To operate core app features and provide personalized functionality.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored using Supabase infrastructure (PostgreSQL database and object storage). Supabase provides industry-standard protection measures for storing and transferring data. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request the deletion of your account and associated content at any time. When your account is deleted, your personal data and user-generated content are removed or anonymized from our active systems.

Some data may temporarily remain in backups for a limited period due to technical reasons. These backups are periodically overwritten and are not used for active processing.

5. Third-Party Services

Libreets uses the following third-party service:

  • Supabase – authentication, database storage, and media hosting.

Your data may be processed by this service according to their respective privacy policies. Libreets does not sell or share your personal data with external advertisers.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Libreets application itself does not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. However, Supabase services may use strictly necessary cookies to enable secure authentication and basic app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Libreets is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw your consent by deleting your account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we may notify you inside the app or by other reasonable means. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your data, please contact us:
libreets@gmail.com
