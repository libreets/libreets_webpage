Libreets – Privacy Policy

Draft version. This policy applies to the educational/test version of the application.

Effective date: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following types of data:

  • Email address provided during account registration.
  • Public profile data such as username, description (bio), and avatar.
  • Content you create inside the Libreets application (e.g., posts, audio, descriptions, etc.).
  • Basic technical logs used to improve stability (non-personal diagnostic information).

2. Why We Collect This Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To display your profile and content to other Libreets users.
  • To operate core app features and provide personalized functionality.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure (PostgreSQL database and object storage). Supabase provides industry-standard protection measures for storing and transferring data.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request the deletion of your account and associated content at any time. When your account is deleted, all personal data and user-generated content are permanently removed.

5. Third-Party Services

Libreets uses the following third-party service:

  • Supabase – authentication, database storage, and media hosting.

Your data may be processed by these services according to their respective privacy policies. Libreets does not sell or share your personal data with external advertisers.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Libreets application itself does not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. However, Supabase services may use strictly necessary cookies to enable secure authentication.

7. Children's Privacy

Libreets is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
libreets@gmail.com
